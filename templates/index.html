Machine Learning Traffic Predictions

All values must be integers. Corresponding integers are below:
Road: any freeway/highway number
Direction: 0-3 (West, South, East, North, respectively)
Day of Week: 0-6 (Monday to Sunday)
Time of Day: 0-24 (Military time in hours)
Status: 0-10 (empty - traffic deadlock)

Example: [10, 2, 3, 15] => [5] refers to moderate traffic on the 10-East on Thursday at 3:00PM.
